Dosa

Details

Dosa is also a south indian food prepared from fermanted rice batter it is also served during breakfast usually served with sambar and chutney with these they make a heavenly combo along with a vada

Ingredients

  • pan
  • ghee
  • dosa batter

steps to make the recepie

  1. heat the pan in medium flame
  2. let the ghee melt
  3. pour the batter in pan
  4. flip the batter once it solidified
