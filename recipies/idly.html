Idly

Description

Idly is most common south indian food it is mostly partaken in breakfast prepared from fermented rice batter it is prepared by steam boiling it is actually healthy

Ingredients

  • fermented batter
  • Rice batter
  • Utensils

Steps to make the receipie

  1. make the batter from grinding the rice
  2. Let it fermant for 12hrs
  3. Set up the Utensils add water and boil
  4. Serve the food
